My Cats

About

Wobbly

Wobbly is recently two years. A Siberean cat who is friendly and playful, and can't stay away from trouble. He also wobbles when he eats.

Moose

Moose is a 3 1/2 year-old short-hair rescue cat. He is very affectionate with those he knows, but is nervous and skittish around those who he doesn't.

Precious

Sibling of Moose from the same litter. She is the only girl in the group so she doesn't get pushed around. Her and Wobbly have beef with each other.

Facts

Here are some facts about our cats!

Fact 1:

Wobbly has an appetite for human food. He enjoys popcorn, oatmeal, and hummus just to name a few.

Fact 2:

Wobbly is called "wobbly" because when he eats or plays his head wobbles aggresively (it's not medically serious).

Fact 3:

Moose's name is often shortened to Moo because of his cow pattern.

Fact 4:

Precious weighs 15 pounds.

Fact 5:

Despite not being realted, Moose and Wobbly share a brotherly bond, and will often rest and look for each other.

Fact 6:

All the cats are indoor cats; although Wobbly jumps out a window and cries outside sometimes.
